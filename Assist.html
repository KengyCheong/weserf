How can I remember the things I read and learn?

7/7/2023AMYuses

It is almost my exam period, and is there anyway to remember what I have read and learned during class or revision?

Helen Gilbert7/7/2023

There are several techniques you can use to improve your ability to remember information that you read and learn. One effective technique is to take notes while you read or listen to information. This can help you to retain the information better by giving you a way to review it later. Another technique is to try to actively engage with the information as you read or listen to it. This can involve asking yourself questions about the material, making connections to other information you already know, or trying to summarize the information in your own words. Finally, it can be helpful to review the information multiple times, either by re-reading or re-listening to it, or by practicing recalling the information from memory.
